---
title: 'Create a blank room
description: 'How to Create a New Blank Room in Heybase.'
---
To create a new blank room in our platform, follow these steps:

1. Go to the Dashboard by logging into your account.
2. Locate the "Create New" button or find the option to select "Blank Room" from the available templates. Click on it.

![https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/912142673/d29c44ab3a7c6a2d5a0b6934/Screenshot+2023-12-18+at+2.31.57%E2%80%AFPM.png](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/912142673/d29c44ab3a7c6a2d5a0b6934/Screenshot+2023-12-18+at+2.31.57%E2%80%AFPM.png)

This will take you to the Editor screen, where you can customize your room.
